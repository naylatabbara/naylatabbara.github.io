Hi! I'm Nayla

  • I’m a 28 year old Lebanese, born and raised in Beirut, Lebanon. I grew up enjoying the country’s rich culture; the different neighborhoods engaged my love for architecture and design.
  • I worked in architecture, however, my interest shifted to branding, storytelling, graphic, and UX/UI design, so I chose to move to Milan to pursue a master’s degree in this field. I’m interested in building brand identities and designing websites.
  • I fluently speak, write and read Arabic, French and English, and I have a B1 in Italian.
  • I enjoy spending time with my family and friends, baking, watching movies, going to the gym, and travelling, getting to meet people from different backgrounds and learning about their culture.
